MEET JADE WEBB

A Natural Eye, Honed by Experience

A Chicago native now based in Atlanta, GA, Jade Webb has been passionately capturing life through a lens for over nine years. Her journey began in 2016, driven by a natural talent for composition and a desire to tell compelling visual stories. This innate gift was further refined through dedicated photography and Photoshop courses at Harold Washington College in Chicago. Today, Jade brings that unique perspective to every client at Blue Lens Photography. She has built a reputation for excellence, with a flawless track record and a business grown on referrals and repeat clients. Whether she's navigating a fast-paced racetrack, a beautiful wedding, or a creative brand shoot, her focus remains the same: to deliver stunning, authentic images that you will treasure.

  • Our Guiding Philosophy

    We believe a great photograph does more than just capture a moment; it preserves a feeling. Our goal is to create images that are not only visually stunning but are also rich with the authentic emotion and energy of your unique story.

  • The Client Experience

    The best images come from genuine comfort and confidence. We prioritize creating a relaxed, collaborative, and enjoyable atmosphere, ensuring you feel completely at ease in front of the lens so your true personality can shine through.
